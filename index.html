My UX Skills

UX Research

Proficiency in planning, conducting, and analyzing various user research methods, such as interviews, surveys, usability testing, card sorting, and field studies. This involves the ability to identify research objectives, recruit participants, create research protocols, and gather qualitative and quantitative data.

Information Architecture

Proficiency in organizing and structuring information to optimize usability and findability. This includes creating sitemaps, user flows, and navigation systems that facilitate intuitive user interactions and seamless content discovery.

Empathy and User Advocacy

A deep understanding and empathy for users, advocating for their needs and ensuring their perspectives are considered throughout the design process. UX researchers often serve as the voice of the user, bridging the gap between users and the design team.
